The Label Philosophy

Independent & Proud

Zero Memories Faithful

a DIY record label that's really just me making too much music under different names. no rules, no genre limitations, just pure creative freedom. everything is recorded, mixed, and released from the same setup. raw, unfiltered, and unapologetically experimental.

Why So Many Projects?

because one outlet isn't enough. each project scratches a different creative itch. sometimes i wanna make peaceful ambient, sometimes i wanna make your ears bleed with hyperpop. why choose when you can do both?

The Process

bedroom producer aesthetic. everything's done in-house with whatever gear i have lying around. imperfections are features, not bugs. lo-fi is a lifestyle, not a limitation.
